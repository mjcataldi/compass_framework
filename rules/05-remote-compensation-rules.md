# 05 — Remote and Compensation Rules

This file defines how STRIDE handles remote-work and compensation risk.

## Remote-Work Assessment

STRIDE should identify whether a role is:

- Fully remote
- Remote within a specific country or region
- Hybrid
- On-site
- Unclear

If the user's workflow requires remote-only roles, flag hybrid, on-site, travel-heavy, or location-constrained roles as a risk.

## Remote Risk Signals

Flag:

- "Remote" but tied to a specific metro area
- Required regular office attendance
- Unclear travel expectations
- Client-site language
- Government or cleared-work constraints that may imply location requirements
- Role descriptions that contradict the posted remote status

## Compensation Assessment

When compensation information is available, evaluate:

- Whether the range appears aligned with role level
- Whether the range appears low for staff-level scope
- Whether contract, W2, C2C, or salaried structure changes the effective value
- Whether benefits, equity, bonus, or public-sector stability affect the tradeoff

## Compensation Risk Signals

Flag:

- Staff-level expectations with mid-level pay
- Principal-level scope without principal-level compensation
- Broad ownership without matching authority or pay
- Contract roles with weak rate for senior scope
- Low ranges hidden behind vague title inflation

## Tone

Be direct, not dramatic. Compensation and remote risk should support decision-making, not derail the whole analysis unless the mismatch is material.

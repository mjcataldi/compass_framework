# Pass Example

This example shows the shape of a pass recommendation.

## Role Fit Summary

Weak fit. The role requires direct experience that is not supported by the source resume, and the missing requirements appear central rather than optional.

## 10-Second Hiring Manager Scan

The candidate may appear senior, but the resume would not quickly answer the hiring manager's primary concern: direct evidence of the specialized experience required for the role.

## ATS and Semantic Alignment Matrix

| Requirement | Evidence | Strength | Notes |
|---|---|---:|---|
| Required specialized domain | Not present in source resume | Missing | Do not claim it |
| Required proprietary platform | Not present in source resume | Missing | High-risk gap |
| General architecture | Source resume may support architecture experience | Moderate/Strong | Not enough to overcome hard requirement |
| Stakeholder communication | Source resume supports communication | Strong | Helpful, but secondary |

## TruthGuard Notes

The role's required specialization cannot be safely inferred from adjacent experience.

## Recommendation

Pass.

The opportunity is not worth a tailored resume unless the user can confirm direct experience that is not currently present in the source resume.

# Cautious Apply Example

This example shows the shape of a cautious STRIDE recommendation.

## Role Fit Summary

Moderate fit with meaningful risk. The candidate has relevant transferable experience, but the role appears to require direct specialization in a domain or stack that is only partially supported by the source resume.

## 10-Second Hiring Manager Scan

The resume may read as senior and technically strong, but the hiring manager may question whether the candidate has enough direct domain experience for this specific role.

## ATS and Semantic Alignment Matrix

| Requirement | Evidence | Strength | Notes |
|---|---|---:|---|
| Specialized domain | Source resume shows adjacent regulated or public-sector experience | Weak/Moderate | Do not claim direct domain experience unless confirmed |
| Cloud architecture | Source resume supports architecture experience | Strong | Useful mitigation |
| Stakeholder leadership | Source resume supports cross-functional leadership | Strong | Emphasize this carefully |
| Specific product stack | Source resume may not show direct hands-on experience | Missing/Weak | Flag as gap |

## TruthGuard Notes

Do not convert adjacent modernization experience into direct specialized-domain experience. Use transferable language instead.

## Recommendation

Apply cautiously.

This may be worth pursuing if the candidate is comfortable with the gap and the resume frames transferable modernization, architecture, and stakeholder leadership experience honestly.
